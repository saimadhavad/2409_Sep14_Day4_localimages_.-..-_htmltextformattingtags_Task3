Most Visited Temples in Andhra Pradesh

1. Kanaka Durga Temple | Vijayawada

While there, offer prayers in front of the revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Durga and take some time to yourself. According to legend, Arjuna received the Pasupata Astra here following his heroic penance offered to Lord Shiva.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.
Temple Timings: 6 am to 11 pm - Every day.
How to Reach: Vijayawada city, accessible by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Vijayawada Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Vijayawada Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Durga Remple Bus Station.

2. Paritala Anjaneya Temple | Vijayawada

Installed in 2003, the statue stands 135 feet tall. Though another monument at Manav Bharti University in Solan stands 155 feet and two inches tall, this one is supposedly the world's second-tall Hanuman statue.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.
Temple Timings: 6 am to 7 pm - Every day.
How to Reach: Vijayawada city, accessible by road.
Nearby Railway Station: RYP/Rayanapadu Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Vijayawada Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Vijayawada Bus stop.

3. Tirumal Venkateswara Temple | Tirumala

Start your pilgrimage through Andhra Pradesh with the Sri Venkateswara temple. From Tirupati, where you can catch a flight, you can take a taxi to the temple. The temple's patron saint is Lord Venkateswara, a form of Vishnu.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.
Temple Timings: 6:30 am to 7:30 pm - Every day.
How to Reach: Tirupati city, accessible by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Tirupati Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Tirupati Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Balaji Bus Stand.

4. Srisailam Mallikarjuna Temple | Kurnool

The religious significance stems from the fact that it is devoted to Lord Shiva and holds one of his twelve Jyotirlingas. You will be enthralled by the four gopurams, or entry pillars, in addition to the temple's holiness.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.
Temple Timings: 6 am to 8 pm - Every day.
How to Reach: Nandyal and Kurnool, accessible by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Markapur Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Kurnool Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: MGBS Bus station.

5. Yaganti Temple | Nandyal

The Nandi deity in front of the Kurnool temple is said by devotees to be constantly growing in size. The Shiva and Parvati idols are among the other main draws here, but the Nandi idol is the main one.

Best Time to Visit: October to November.
Temple Timings: 6 am to 1 pm and 3 pm to 7:30 pm - Every day.
How to Reach: Kurnooll City, Banaganapalle-Peapully road, accessible by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Kazipet Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Tirupathi Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Yaganti Bus Station.
