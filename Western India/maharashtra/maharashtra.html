Most Visited Temples in Maharashtra

1. Shree Siddhivinayak Temple | Mumbai

In the heart of Mumbai, the Siddhivinayak Temple is a haven of peace and spirituality devoted to the venerated god Ganesha. Devotees from all over the world come here in search of blessings and heavenly grace because of its majestic architecture and calm atmosphere.

Best Time to Visit: September to February.
Temple Timings: Open daily from 5:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
How to Reach: Easily accessible by local trains and taxis.
Nearby Railway Station: Dadar Station.
Nearby Airport: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Siddhivinayak Temple Bus Stop.

2. Shirdi Sai Baba Temple | Shirdi

The Shirdi Sai Baba Temple is a spiritual sanctuary devoted to the venerated saint Sai Baba, and it is tucked away in the charming town of Shirdi. Enveloped in mystique and reverence, this sacred abode exudes an aura of peace, attracting devotees of spiritual enlightenment.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.
Temple Timings: Open daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
How to Reach: Well-connected by road and rail.
Nearby Railway Station: Sainagar Shirdi Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Shirdi International Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Shirdi Bus Depot.

3. Trimbakeshwar Temple | Nashik

Trimbakeshwar Temple, nestled amidst the scenic beauty of Nashik, is a sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. With its intricate architecture and spiritual significance, it serves as a pilgrimage site for devotees seeking blessings and divine intervention.

Best Time to Visit: July to September.
Temple Timings: Open daily, 24 hours.
How to Reach: Well-connected by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Nashik Road Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Nashik International Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Trimbakeshwar Bus Stand.

4. Mahalakshmi Temple | Kolhapur

Mahalakshmi Temple, situated in the historic city of Kolhapur, is a revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. Its ornate architecture and spiritual aura attract devotees seeking blessings and solace.

Best Time to Visit: October to February.
Temple Timings: Open daily from 4:30 AM to 10:00 PM.
How to Reach: Palitana is accessible by road. From there, pilgrims can climb the stairs or opt for a ropeway ride to reach the temples.
Nearby Railway Station: Kolhapur Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Kolhapur Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Kolhapur Central Bus Stand

5. Grishneshwar Temple | Ellora

Grishneshwar Temple, nestled amidst the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Ellora Caves, is a sacred abode dedicated to Lord Shiva. Renowned for its architectural splendour and spiritual significance, it attracts devotees and history enthusiasts alike.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.
Temple Timings: Open daily from 5:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
How to Reach: Well connected by road.
Nearby Railway Station: Aurangabad Railway Station.
Nearby Airport: Aurangabad Airport.
Nearby Bus Station: Ellora Bus Stand.
