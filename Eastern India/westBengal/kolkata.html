Most Visited Temples in Kolkata

1. ISKCON Temple | Minto Park

The ISKCON temple, which was constructed in honour of Lord Krishna, is spread out across the nation. In 1970, the ISKCON temple in Kolkata was founded to serve the devotees of Lord Krishna and his partner and everlasting love, Radha.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.
Temple Timings: 4 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. and 4 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. - Every day.
Nearby Places: Asiatic Society, Mother House, South Park Street Cemetery, and Maidan.

2. Sri Bal Hanuman Mandir | Lake Town

The temple's main deity, Lord Hanuman, is depicted in various forms and poses, symbolising different aspects of his character and devotion. The temple's architecture reflects traditional Hindu temple design.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.
Temple Timings: 6 a.m. to 8 p.m. - Every day.
Nearby Places: Manasha Mandir, Lake Town Adhibasi Brindo Durga Puja Pandal.

3. Shirdi Sai Baba Temple | Sodepur

There was no other Sai Baba temple in Kolkata before the establishment of this one in 1913. For all of Sai Baba's followers, this is a significant location of pilgrimage and worship and one of the most popular temples nowadays.

Best Time to Visit: March to May.
Temple Timings: 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. - Every day.
Nearby Places: Khandoba Mandir, Baba Chavadi, and Lendi Baug.

4. Calcutta Jain Temple | Badridas Temple Street

Also referred to as the Parshwanath Jain Temple, Rai Badridas Bahadur Mookim established it in 1867. This well-known Jain Dharamshala in Kolkata is made up of four separate structures, each of which provides breathtaking insights into the Jain culture.

Best Time to Visit: August to September.
Temple Timings: 6 a.m. to 11:30 p.m. and 3 p.m. to 7 p.m. - Every day.
Nearby Places: Victoria Memorial, Fort William, Asutosh Museum of Indian Art, and Armenian Church.

5. Kalighat Temple | Khalighat

The Bengali population has great regard for Kalighat Mandir due to their tremendous respect for all versions of Goddess Durga. If you were unaware, Goddess Kali is regarded as one of the avatars.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.
Temple Timings: 5 a.m. to 2 p.m. and 5 p.m. to 10:30 p.m.- Every day.
Nearby Places: Mahanayak Uttam Mancha, Netaji Bhawan, and Kolkata Zoo & Zoological Garden.
